The problem

Many clinics lose inquiries without realizing it.

Calls go to voicemail. Contact forms sit unanswered. Potential patients move on to the next clinic that responds faster. Most clinics do not have a lead problem. They have an intake problem.

Missed calls

Calls during treatments, after hours, or during busy front-desk moments often go unanswered.

Slow follow-up

Manual follow-up makes it harder to respond quickly when a new inquiry is ready to book.

Booking friction

If the next step is unclear or scheduling feels difficult, potential patients often abandon the process.

Common questions

Is this a full practice management system?

No. It is focused on intake flow, follow-up, and booking support. It does not replace your EHR or billing software.

What software does it use?

Usually a form tool, a scheduling tool, and an automation layer. The exact stack can vary based on what your clinic already uses.

Do you run ads?

No. Alignment Automations focuses on helping clinics handle inquiries more effectively once those inquiries already exist.

Can we add missed-call text back or a website later?

Yes. The system is modular, so you can start with the core layer and expand when needed.

Who owns the accounts?

Your clinic does. That keeps the setup portable and makes the relationship safer for you long term.

Does this replace my front desk?

No. It reduces repetitive intake work and helps your team respond more consistently. It is meant to support your staff, not replace them.

What if we want to cancel?

You can cancel support. Because the clinic owns the accounts, forms, and data, the system is not locked away from you.
