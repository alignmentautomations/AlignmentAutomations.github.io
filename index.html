Common Questions

Is this a full practice management system?

No — it's focused specifically on online inquiry response and appointment booking. It doesn't replace your EHR or billing software.

What software does it use?

Typically a form tool (like Tally or Typeform), a scheduling tool (like Calendly), and an automation layer (like Make or Zapier). You're not locked in — tools can be swapped if needed.

Do you run ads?

No. We build intake infrastructure that converts the leads you already receive.

Can we add Call Capture or a website later?

Yes. The system is modular — start with the Core and add layers when your clinic is ready.

Who owns the accounts?

Your clinic does. You create the accounts; Alignment Automations is added as an admin to build and manage everything. This means you're never locked in — you can take over or work with someone else at any time.

Does this replace my front desk?

No — it reduces repetitive follow-up so your staff can focus on patients already in the clinic. It handles new online inquiries automatically.

How long does setup take?

Most setups are complete within 5–7 business days after your clinic provides access and approves the flow.

What if we want to cancel?

Cancel any time. Since you own the accounts, you keep everything — forms, calendar, and data.
