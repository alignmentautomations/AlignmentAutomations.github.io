The problem

Many contractors lose jobs without realizing it.

Contact forms go unanswered. New inquiries wait hours for a response. Potential customers move on to the next contractor that gets back to them first. Most contractors do not have a lead problem. They have an intake problem.

Unanswered inquiries

Contact forms and online inquiries sit without a response while potential customers move on to the next contractor.

Slow follow-up

Manual follow-up makes it harder to respond quickly when a new inquiry is ready to book.

Booking friction

If the next step is unclear or scheduling feels difficult, potential patients often abandon the process.

Common questions

Is this a full practice management system?

No. It is focused on intake flow, follow-up, and booking support. It does not replace your EHR or billing software.

What software does it use?

Usually a form tool, a scheduling tool, and an automation layer. The exact stack can vary based on what your business already uses.

Do you run ads?

No. Alignment Automations focuses on helping contractors handle inquiries more effectively once those inquiries already exist.

Can we start with one product and add the other later?

Yes. Start with the Automation Engine if you have an existing site, or Website Infrastructure if you need one built. Both can be combined into the Website Infrastructure + Automation Engine bundle when you're ready.

Who owns the accounts?

You do. That keeps the setup portable and makes the relationship safer for you long term.

Does this replace my office staff?

No. It reduces repetitive intake work and helps your team respond more consistently. It is meant to support your staff, not replace them.

What if we want to cancel?

You can cancel support. Because you own the accounts, forms, and data, the system is not locked away from you.
