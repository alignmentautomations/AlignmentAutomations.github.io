Pricing Questions

What's included in "system management"?

Monthly system management covers ongoing monitoring, small edits as needed, workflow maintenance, and support with access or billing setup. You're not on your own after launch.

Do I need the Foundation if I already have a website?

No — the Automation Core works with your existing website. Foundation is only needed if you don't have a site or if your current one isn't structured for intake and booking.

Can I start with Core and add Call Capture later?

Yes. The system is modular — start with what your clinic needs now and add layers when you're ready. Bundled pricing applies when both are set up together.

Are there any tool costs beyond these prices?

Yes — the tools your system runs on (form software, scheduling, automation layer) are billed directly to your clinic. These typically run under $50/month in total and are completely transparent.

What if I want to cancel?

Cancel anytime. Since you own all the accounts, you keep everything — your forms, calendar, data, and website. There's no lock-in.

How long does setup take?

Most setups are complete within 5–7 business days after your clinic provides access and approves the flow.
