Pricing Questions

What's included in "system management"?

Monthly system management covers ongoing monitoring, small edits as needed, workflow maintenance, and support with access or billing setup. You're not on your own after launch.

Do I need Website Infrastructure if I already have a website?

No — the Automation Engine works with your existing website. Website Infrastructure is only needed if you don't have a site or if your current one isn't structured for intake and booking.

Can I start with just the Automation Engine and add a website later?

Yes. Start with the Automation Engine if you already have a working site. When you're ready to build or rebuild your site, both can be combined under the Website Infrastructure + Automation Engine bundle at a lower total setup cost.

Are there any tool costs beyond these prices?

Yes — the tools your system runs on (form software, scheduling, automation layer) are billed directly to your business. These typically run under $50/month in total and are completely transparent.

What if I want to cancel?

Cancel anytime. Since you own all the accounts, you keep everything — your forms, calendar, data, and website. There's no lock-in.

How long does setup take?

Most setups are complete within 5–7 business days after you provide access and approve the flow.
